What We're Looking For

Not every church near the A1 belongs in this collection. We prioritize:

  1. Historical significance ‚Äî Churches with unusual history, famous connections, or important architectural features
  2. Proximity to the route ‚Äî Churches visible from the road, or within easy detour distance
  3. Accessibility ‚Äî Churches that can actually be visited (at least externally)
